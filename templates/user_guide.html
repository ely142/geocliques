FAQ

How can I delete my account?

Sad to see you go! You can delete your account by going to the Settings page and selecting the Manage Account button. You'll be asked to enter your password and confirm it to proceed.

Please note: Deleting your account is permanent. All your data, including any markers you've created, reviews, events, and cliques that you are their admin will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

I'm the admin of a clique - how can I keep it from being deleted when I delete my account?

If your clique is valuable to you and its members, but you still wish to delete your account, you can transfer ownership to another trusted member.
To do this:

  1. Go to the Admin Control Room page of the clique.
  2. Use the üîç in the members table to find the member you want to make the new admin.
  3. Click the Make Admin Instead button.
  4. Fill in the required confirmation details.

What happens when I report a member in the clique I run?

When an admin reports a user from their clique, the report is sent to our team for review. We will analyze the user's activity in that clique and others to determine the appropriate action. If you also wish to ban the user from your clique, be sure to submit the report first.

How can I quickly find specific information in the tables on the platform?

You can use the üîç icon that appears next to certain fields in the tables. It allows you to search and filter the content, making it easier and faster to find exactly what you're looking for.
